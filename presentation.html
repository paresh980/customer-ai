Call Transcription & Auto-Triage

Automatic speech-to-text on every call recording, followed by NLP classification into Closed / Open / Urgent buckets. Enables instant priority routing and SLA tracking.

⏱ 60% faster triage 📉 Fewer missed escalations
🤖

AI Chatbot for FAQs & Troubleshooting

24/7 conversational assistant trained on Smartnode's product catalog (touch switches, IR blasters, curtain controllers). Resolves common setup & pairing queries instantly.

🔄 40% ticket deflection 🌙 24/7 availability

Real-Time Sentiment Analysis

Analyze live calls, emails & chats for negative sentiment to auto-escalate at-risk interactions. Tracks CSAT trends across product lines and dealer regions.

Predictive Maintenance Alerts

ML models trained on historical ticket data to predict device failures (e.g., switch connectivity drops). Proactively notify customers & dealers before issues arise.

Dashboard tracking dealer-level complaint rates, resolution quality & product return patterns. ML-powered demand forecasting to optimize inventory across regions.
